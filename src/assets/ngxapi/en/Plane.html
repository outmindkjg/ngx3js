Plane

A two dimensional surface that extends infinitely in 3d space, represented in [link:http://mathworld.wolfram.com/HessianNormalForm.html Hessian normal form]
by a unit length normal vector and a constant.

Constructor

[constructor:Plane Plane]( [param:Vector3 normal]?, [param:number constant]? )

Parameters

[parameter:Vector3 normal] - (optional) a unit length [Vector3](https://outmindkjg.github.io/ngx3js-doc/#/docs/ngxapi/en/Vector3) defining the normal of the plane. Default is *(1, 0, 0)*.
[parameter:number constant] - (optional) the signed distance from the origin to the plane. Default is *0*.
