Ray

A ray that emits from an origin in a certain direction. This is used by the [page:Raycaster Raycaster] to assist with [link:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ray_casting raycasting].e Raycasting is used for mouse picking (working out what objects in the 3D space the mouse is over) amongst other things.
See the [page:Ray ngx3js docs] page for details.

[constructor:Ray Ray]( [param:Vector3 origin]?, [param:Vector3 direction]? )

Parameters

[parameter:Vector3 origin] - (optional) The origin of the [page:Ray Ray]. Default is a [page:Vector3 Vector3] at (0, 0, 0).
[parameter:Vector3 direction] - (optional) Vector3 The direction of the [page:Ray Ray]. This must be normalized (with [page:Vector3.normalize Vector3.normalize]) for the methods to operate properly. Default is a [page:Vector3 Vector3] at (0, 0, -1).

Creates a new Ray.
