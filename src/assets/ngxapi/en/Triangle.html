Triangle

A geometric triangle as defined by three [page:Vector3 Vector3s] representing its three corners.

Constructor

[constructor:Triangle Triangle]( [param:Vector3 a]?, [param:Vector3 b]?, [param:Vector3 c]? )

Parameters

[parameter:Vector3 a] - (optional) the first corner of the triangle. Default is a [Vector3](https://outmindkjg.github.io/ngx3js-doc/#/docs/ngxapi/en/Vector3) at (0, 0, 0).
[parameter:Vector3 b] - (optional) the second corner of the triangle. Default is a [Vector3](https://outmindkjg.github.io/ngx3js-doc/#/docs/ngxapi/en/Vector3) at (0, 0, 0).
[parameter:Vector3 c] - (optional) the final corner of the triangle. Default is a [Vector3](https://outmindkjg.github.io/ngx3js-doc/#/docs/ngxapi/en/Vector3) at (0, 0, 0).

Creates a new Triangle.

Properties

The second corner of the triangle. Default is a [page:Vector3 Vector3] at (0, 0, 0).

[property:Vector3 c]

the final corner of the triangle. Default is a [page:Vector3 Vector3] at (0, 0, 0)
