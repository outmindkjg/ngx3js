Spherical

A point's [link:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spherical_coordinate_system spherical coordinates].

Constructor

[constructor:Spherical Spherical]( [param:number radius]?, [param:number phi]?, [param:number theta]? )

Parameters

[parameter:number radius] - (optional) The radius, or the [link:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euclidean_distance Euclidean distance] (straight-line distance) from the point to the origin. Default is 1.0.
[parameter:number phi] - (optional) polar angle in radians from the y (up) axis. Default is 0.
[parameter:number theta] - (optional) The theta.

The poles (phi) are at the positive and negative y axis. The equator (theta) starts at positive z.

Properties

[property:number phi]

Polar angle in radians from the y (up) axis. Default is 0.

[property:number radius]

The radius, or the [link:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euclidean_distance Euclidean distance] (straight-line distance) from the point to the origin. Default is 1.0.

[property:number theta]

Equator angle in radians around the y (up) axis. Default is 0.
